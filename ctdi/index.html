CTDI Europe · AI Service & Supply-Chain Workforce
AI operators that win contracts, trade equipment, triage repairs, optimise spare-parts stocking and run test engineering
Independent concept  ·  illustrative
Each AI operator is a commercial or service-operations workflow turned into an autonomous team of agents. One track grows the business: it wins carrier, cable and OEM contracts, runs the Supply equipment-trading desk, and does market intelligence. The other track runs the service floor: it triages a returned unit and diagnoses the fault, optimises forward stocking across the spare-parts network, builds the test-engineering programs, and analyses failures for quality. Point one at a contract, a returned unit or the spares network and it runs the loop, then hands back a review-ready output for an engineer or manager to approve.
