AI operators that find customers, license the dry-electrode tech, size ultracapacitor systems, chase grants, and run the line
Independent concept  ·  illustrative
Each AI operator is a commercial or technical workflow turned into an autonomous team of agents. One track grows the business: it finds customers across grid, data center, EV and industrial, develops licensing and partnerships for the Activated Dry Electrode process, and runs market intelligence and outreach. A second track is technical and operations: it sizes ultracapacitor systems for a use case, navigates grants and incentives, watches line quality and yield, and plans materials and supply. Point one at an account or a project and it runs the loop, then hands back a review-ready output for your team to approve.
