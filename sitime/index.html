AI operators that find design wins, cross-reference quartz to MEMS, size timing budgets, qualify parts and protect supply
Independent concept  ·  illustrative
Each AI operator is a commercial or applications-engineering workflow turned into an autonomous team of agents. One track wins designs: it finds programs that need precision timing across AI data centers, 5G, automotive and aerospace, quotes them, and runs market and competitive intelligence. The other track is applications engineering and operations: it cross-references an incumbent quartz part to a programmable MEMS equivalent, sizes the timing budget, assembles the qualification evidence, and protects supply and allocation. Point one at an account or a part number and it runs the loop, then hands back a review-ready output for an engineer to approve.
